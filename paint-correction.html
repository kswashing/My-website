What Is Paint Correction?

Swirls Gone. Paint Restored.

Over time, your vehicle's paint develops swirl marks from improper washing, fine scratches, water spots, and oxidation from UV exposure. These imperfections scatter light and make your paint look dull, hazy, and faded.

Paint correction uses professional machine polishing to permanently remove these defects from your clear coat — revealing the deep, reflective, mirror-like finish that's hidden underneath. It's one of the most dramatic transformations in auto detailing.

Option 01

One-Step Paint Correction

Starting at $499

Ideal for vehicles with mild to moderate swirl marks, light scratches, and water spots. A single stage of machine polishing removes most surface-level imperfections and dramatically improves gloss.

  • ✓  Removes mild swirl marks & light scratches
  • ✓  Eliminates water spots
  • ✓  Restores paint gloss and clarity
  • ✓  Includes hand wash and decontamination

Option 02

Two-Step Paint Correction

Starting at $699

For vehicles with heavy swirl marks, deeper scratches, oxidation, or neglected paint. A two-stage process — cutting stage removes defects, polishing stage refines the finish to maximum clarity.

  • ✓  Removes heavy swirls & deeper scratches
  • ✓  Corrects oxidation and paint fade
  • ✓  Maximum paint clarity and reflection
  • ✓  Includes full decontamination wash
  • ✓  Ideal before ceramic coating

Pair With Ceramic Coating

We strongly recommend doing paint correction before applying a ceramic coating. Ceramic coating locks in whatever is under it — including scratches and swirls. Correct the paint first, then protect it for the best possible long-term result.
