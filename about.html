Our Story

Built on Passion & Pride

KS Washing was founded by Kaleb Sherrets with a simple mission — bring professional-grade detailing directly to your driveway. We got into this business because we genuinely love cars and take pride in the results we deliver.

Based in Independence, Iowa, we serve customers throughout Manchester, Independence, and surrounding communities in Delaware and Buchanan County. Mobile detailing means no more driving somewhere, waiting around, and driving back. We come to you, whether that's your home, your workplace, or anywhere else that works.

Every vehicle gets our full attention and effort. We don't rush jobs, we don't cut corners, and we don't leave until the work is done right. Your satisfaction is guaranteed.
